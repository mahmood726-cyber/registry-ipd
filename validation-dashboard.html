Registry-IPD — Validation Dashboard

Reconstructing pseudo individual-patient survival data from ClinicalTrials.gov / AACT summary data only, validated up to true patient-level RCT data. Fully offline. See VALIDATION.md / METHODS.md in the repo for full detail and caveats.

Headline verdict (honest)

For adequately-sized trials, RMST and median reconstruct to ~2–6% and the HR to ~11% (median); the 95% credible interval covers the true HR 14/14 on real data. HR is the hard estimand; very small trials don't reconstruct; needs ≥5–6 posted KM timepoints. Not a substitute for true IPD — a triangulation tool with honest uncertainty.
